General Educations Coursework

The courses that I have taken towards my general education requriement includes coursework in: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Calculus 1 and 2, Differential Equations, Linear Algebra, Speech, and Technical Writing.

Impact of General Education on Degree

My general education has impacted my education because they make me a more rounded individual. I feel that I have a better understanding of the world around me and how I can better work with different people and use different general education skills to solve engineering real world problems. Through the Biology courses I took I obtained a Minor in Biology. The Physics has been useful with understanding concepts relative to Physics and their application in the real world. The math courses I took have definitely been applicable to my degree and Software Engineering career because of the use of algorithms, logic, physics applications, and computer graphics. The speech and technical writing courses were helpful because they gave me a understanding of good technical writing skills as well as public speaking skills. All of these skills obtained through general education have been beneficial to my coursework. They have taught me many different things varying from gaining new study habits to technical skills applied everyday in Engineering problems.
